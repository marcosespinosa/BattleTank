gdd (game design document)

concept:

You control a tank in a 3d space. You shoot at other tanks controlled by the AI while you avoid being hit by them.

rules:

You win the game by shooting at the other tanks and getting rid of their health before that happens to you.

requirements:

3d environment
3d model for tank
3d model for bullet
particle effect for explosion + fire
sfx for shooting
sfx for moving
sfx for taking a hit
img for home menu
buttons for home menu
song for home menu
sfx for hover on buttons in home menu
